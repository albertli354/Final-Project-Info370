From the image above, we can see how validation accuracy changed with the number of epoches and number of writers in the dataset. From the data, as the number of writers in the dataset increased, the accuracy score decreased proportionally.

a five-writers datset, the validation accuracy could reach at 76% and the train accuracy can even at 90%. However, when the number of writers increased to 30, validation accuracy only decreased to 43% and train accuracy decreased to 81%. As the number of writers increased in the dataset, it is really hard for the model to calculate all the subtle differences.

intersting point is the relationship between the number of epoches and accuracy.Both of the four models experienced a dramatically increase in accuracy from the beginning to the second round. However, for the validation accuracy, the accuracy score seems not changed much, and even experience small decreases when the number of epoches increased (see the image below).
